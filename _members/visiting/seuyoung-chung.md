Visiting Student, Department of Biomedical Informatics, School of Medicine, Emory University

I'm a recent Georgia Tech CS graduate with a background in software engineering, product design, and UI/UX. Before joining ViTAL, I worked as a UX designer at early-stage startups. I'm interested in building tools that support people's health and wellness, with an emphasis on whether they're genuinely usable, not just technically impressive. I'm interested in human-computer interaction, specifically usability and accessibility in health technology, and how a system gets presented to the people who actually use it. Coming from design, I care most about closing the gap between something that works in a lab and something a patient or clinician can pick up and use.
